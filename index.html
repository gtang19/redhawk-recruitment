Redhawk Ventures Fall 2020

What is Redhawk Ventures

Redhawk Ventures is a student run venture capital fund, that invests up to $25,000 into startups founded by Miami students and alumni.

The fund currently has 24 members, and is looking to expand in the Fall 2020 semester.

What "type" of club is Redhawk Ventures

Redhawk Ventures is a club that focuses on professional development and experiental learning, while fostering an ambitious environment. Investment analysts on the fund conduct diligence on and talk to real startups, and real founders.

At Redhawk Ventures, we teach new members everything they need to know about venture in their first semester on the club, through a comprehensive new member education. After that, everything learned is learning by doing.

Venture capital is a human focused industry. As a result, it's important for us to maintain an atmosphere that promotes the development of emotional intellegence skills. While Redhawk Ventures is not a "social" club, a strong sense of comradery is developed through the work we do.

Career Development at Redhawk Ventures

Typically, internships in the venture capital field only exist for grad students. However, by leveraging connections to the local entrepreneurship community, members of Redhawk Ventures are consistently placed into competitive internships at venture firms, startups, and other companies.

Redhawk Ventures also puts members in a unique situation where they can dig deep into the business model of startups, and understand how they work. This deeper understanding of business function is beneficial to any career path, financial or otherwise.

Only for Finance Majors?

Redhawk Ventures may sound like a finance club, but VC is more about the people than it is about the money. You don't need to have any financial skills coming into Redhawk Ventures to be successful.

Startups exist in every field of study, and learning about the entrepreneurial ecosystem will strenghten your career path regardless of if you are a finance major or not.

Redhawk Ventures and Covid-19

These are strange times for us as students, and also for the entrepereneurial ecosystem.

Despite the challenges we're all currently facing, we encourage you to apply for Redhawk Ventures. The close ties of Redhawk Ventures to the local startup ecosystem means that we have an opportunity to play a more active role in weathering the storm, and eventually rebuilding in a post-pandemic economy.
